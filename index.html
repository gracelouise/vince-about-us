Our Philosophy

Effortless should not take work.

Putting together an outfit in the morning should be intuitive.

Feeling comfortable in your clothes should be the rule, not the exception.

Looking polished and at ease should never be a novelty.

That's where the idea for our anything-but-basic essentials came from when we first launched in 2002. That's the idea at the forefront of the design of every new collection.

Luxury should never look obvious. It should always come with a sense of ease. This is the philosophy we use in everything we do.

Who We Are

VINCE. was founded in 2002 with the idea that women and men want to feel effortlessly modern every day. Our Iconic Essentials offered beautifully sourced materials in designs that felt perfect any day of the week. While we continue to evolve our designs, our core esthetic remains simple: rooted in the remarkable quality we were first known for, and refreshed with detailing unique to VINCE.

Great style starts with great design. Ours is conceived in our L.A. studio and imagined on the streets of N.Y.
